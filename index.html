Welcome to Joyful Restaurant

At Joyful Restaurant, we serve a variety of both local and international dishes, blending the best of traditional Nigerian flavors with modern culinary techniques.

Iyan (Pounded Yam)

Known for its soft, smooth texture, best served with soups like Egusi or Efo Riro.

Eba

Made from cassava, paired with various soups for a rich, hearty meal.

Jollof Rice

A classic West African dish, spiced rice cooked with tomatoes, onions, and peppers.

Fried Rice

Stir-fried rice with vegetables, often paired with chicken or fish.

Spaghetti

Italian-inspired dish, served with a variety of sauces or stir-fried for a unique twist.

Our Signature Soups

  • Egusi Soup: A hearty, protein-rich soup made from ground melon seeds.
  • Okra Soup: A savory, thick soup often served with fufu or eba.
  • Efo Riro: A popular Nigerian vegetable stew made with spinach or kale, palm oil often served with fufu or iyan

About Us

Joyful Restaurant blends Nigerian tradition with modern tastes, serving a variety of local and international dishes. Our goal is to provide a memorable dining experience, where food, passion, and culture come together in every bite.
